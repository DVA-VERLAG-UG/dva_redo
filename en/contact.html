Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most important questions

01

How long does production take?

From print approval to worldwide availability: maximum 14 days. Other publishers often take months.

02

What does publishing cost?

Costs depend on your chosen package. Use our configurator for a personalised quote or contact us for a free consultation.

03

Do I keep the rights to my book?

Yes, 100%! You retain all rights to your work. We are a service provider, not a publisher in the traditional sense.

04

Will my book be listed on Amazon?

Yes! Your book will be available on Amazon, in German bookshops and on all major online platforms.

05

Do I need a traditional publisher?

No! With DVAYD Publishing you get all the benefits of a publisher without the drawbacks. You keep control and your rights.

06

How does ISBN assignment work?

We assign your German ISBN in Bremen. It is listed in all relevant databases and valid worldwide.

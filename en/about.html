Our Story

From passion
came mission

2005 in Istanbul: What began as a small publishing house grew into a vision — to give authors worldwide access to the professional book market. Without intermediaries. Without compromise. Through direct paths and the highest quality.

We recognised early on: the future of publishing lies in connecting international production with local market access. While other publishers drowned in bureaucracy, we built a pipeline between Turkey's finest printers and the German book trade.

Today: Over 220 authors have found their place in the book market with us. From Amazon to local bookshops — without waiting times, without loss of quality, without compromising on your rights.
